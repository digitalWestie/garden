_The second of two parts, [this video](https://youtu.be/LEMWJN7_ds4) covers the front-end steps in creating a map of the UK's oldest trademarks._

Here we visualise the trademarks data we gathered in [part 1](<%= url_for("_posts/2023-04-19-mapping-the-uks-oldest-trademarks-part-1-preparing-data-with-sqlite-and-python.md") %>) using D3js by rendering a map of the UK with postal area boundaries, labelling and then filling with the oldest trademark for the area.

We begin with this [starter template](https://creating-with-data.glitch.me/trademarks/starter.html), and finish with [this visualisation](https://creating-with-data.glitch.me/trademarks/screencast.html). The map we create in the screencast represents about 70% of the work that went into [the final visualistion](<%= url_for("_posts/2023-04-13-dataviz-the-uks-oldest-trademarks.md") %>) which you can check out over here.

You can find the code for [both steps, plus the final visualisation on Glitch](https://glitch.com/edit/#!/creating-with-data?path=trademarks%2Fstarter.html%3A33%3A7)!

Some of the interactive code examples and demos in older posts on _Creating With Data_ were originally hosted on [Glitch](https://glitch.com/), a platform for building and sharing live code projects.

Recently, Glitch [announced](https://blog.glitch.com/post/changes-are-coming-to-glitch/) that it is shutting down its free hosting services. As a result, many of the embedded demos and linked projects that relied on Glitch may no longer load or function correctly.

I’m currently working on migrating this blog and all the projects I had on Glitch, including my personal website.

I'm going to consolidate many of my personal sites and projects, but it'll probably take me a while. It's quite an undertaking as I've been using Glitch for about 8-9 years now!

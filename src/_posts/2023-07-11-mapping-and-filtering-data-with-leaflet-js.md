In this video, we visualise data from a spreadsheet of doctors' practices and prescriptions adding two methods for filtering. We deal with the issue of combining the two separate filters as one has a knock-on effect on the other.

NB- the **data is synthetic**, it's just modelled on the real thing!

This video is on the long side. If you're more interested in the visualisation than the control/filtering stuff you can just watch the first 20-25 mins!

**Notes**

You can find the [starter template](https://creating-with-data.glitch.me/leaflet-filtering/start.html) and [the finished article on Glitch](https://creating-with-data.glitch.me/leaflet-filtering/complete.html).
